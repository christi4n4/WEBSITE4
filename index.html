WELCOME TO FASHIONISTA

FASHION IS EVERYWHERE. DEFINITION: “Fashion is a dynamic force that ignites our sense of self-worth, enhances our creativity, and reflects our inner beauty”.

FASHION is A SENSE OF ART. A MASTERPIECE that was crafted together from designer to consumer to project creativity as our bodies are the blank canvas.

WHERE

FASHION occurs from within and is then expressed to the outer world. Our use of clothing pieces, accessories and shoes can say a lot about our personalities and interests. IT IS A SENSE OF IDENTITY. Exploring different clothing stores and being open to different styles can help individuals discover their sense of FASHION.

WHY

FASHION can allow your exterior to speak to world rather than your voice. It communicates your identity and is one of the most powerful tools for self-expression. FASHION impacts how we perceive each other and can either help us or delay us from connecting with like-minded people. In that case… TODAY IS THE DAY YOU DISPLAY YOUR SENSE OF FASHION & STYLE IN THE WAY YOU WANT TO BE SEEN. LET IT SPEAK FOR YOU. LET IT BRING OPPORTUNITIES TO CONNECT WITH OTHERS AND EXPRESS YOUR IDENTITY!
